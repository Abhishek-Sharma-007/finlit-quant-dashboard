## What Each Tab Does
- **Single Asset Analysis**: computes core indicators and risk metrics, shows charts, and displays recent/full OHLCV tables with educational signal hypothesis text.
- **Asset Comparison**: compares multiple assets with normalized performance, summary statistics, daily return correlation heatmap, and cross-asset research insight.
- **Portfolio Simulator**: validates ticker/weight input, computes portfolio metrics, shows equity and allocation charts, and provides portfolio risk hypothesis text.
- **Financial Literature Search**: runs TF-IDF similarity over literature notes, displays results as cards and dataframe, and adds literature-to-signal educational insight.
- **Report**: generates downloadable markdown report with metrics, explanations, hypotheses, literature matches, and educational disclaimer.

## Educational Note
All outputs are historical observations for learning and research hypothesis generation. They require validation and are not financial advice.
